    • CHECKING ORDER STATUS

      When shopping on www.evebyeves.com, items added to your shopping cart will remain there until they are purchased, sold out or removed from your session . When you are ready to purchase, you can view your shopping bag and proceed to check out. When you register with us, your shipping and billing information is saved, which will make your checkout process faster next time. You can track your order status and order history in the My Account section.

      Please note that we reserve the right to review all orders placed on our site. Once your order has been submitted you will receive an email notification indicating the status of your order. If your order is under review you will receive a subsequent email indicating the results within one business day. We unfortunately will not have an update prior to that time. Thank you for your patience.

      If you have a promotion code for evebyeves.com, you must enter it in the Promo Code box at checkout once you are ready to make a purchase. Promotion codes can not be used to purchase Gift Cards or Virtual Gift Certificates. Only one promotion code can be used per order, codes cannot be applied to previous purchases and some exclusions apply.

    • CHANGING OR CANCELING YOUR ORDER UNITED STATES

      To cancel or change an order, please contact customer service at: support@evebyeves.com or call 1-888-xxx-xxxx. We will do our best to accommodate your request. If you wish to add merchandise to your order after the order is placed, you must place a new order for the additional merchandise. We will do our best to ship these orders together. At times, however, we will not be able to accommodate this circumstance and will have to ship separately.
